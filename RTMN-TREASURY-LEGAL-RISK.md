---

## Capital Allocation Rules

| Situation | Rule | Amount |
|-----------|------|--------|
| **Company Profitable** | % reinvested in growth | 70% |
| **Company Loss-making** | RTMN covers losses | Up to budget |
| **Shared infra expansion** | RTMN funds | As needed |
| **New product incubation** | RTMN or subsidiary? | Case-by-case |
| **AI infra losses** | Absorbed by REZ Intelligence | Up to ₹1 Cr |
| **Emergency fund** | Maintained by RTMN | ₹2 Cr minimum |

---

## Allocation Decision Matrix

| Scenario | Decision | Funding |
|----------|----------|---------|
| New company starts | RTMN funds initial | ₹50L-1 Cr |
| Company needs more capital | Raise external or RTMN | Case-by-case |
| Shared infra expansion | RTMN funds | 100% |
| AI model training costs | REZ Intelligence | Via internal budget |
| Company raises external | RTMN maintains control (>51%) | - |
| Company wants to spinout | RTMN gets equity stake | Pro-rata |

# PART 4: PRODUCT LIFECYCLE GOVERNANCE

## Product Stages

| Stage | Meaning | Funding | Team | P&L |
|-------|---------|---------|------|------|
| **Incubation** | Internal experiment | RTMN | Shared | Combined |
| **MVP** | Testing with users | RTMN | Dedicated | Separate tracking |
| **Growth** | Scaling | Subsidiary | Dedicated | Breakeven target |
| **Operating Unit** | Mature product | Self-sustaining | Full team | Independent P&L |
| **Spinout** | Independent company | External round | Full team | Own P&L |

---

## Stage Transitions

```
INCUBATION (0-3 months)
 │
 ├── Validated by leadership
 └── Moves to MVP
 │
 ▼
MVP (3-6 months)
 │
 ├── 100 users/merchants
 └── Moves to Growth
 │
 ▼
GROWTH (6-18 months)
 │
 ├── Unit economics proven
 └── Moves to Operating
 │
 ▼
OPERATING UNIT (18+ months)
 │
 ├── Self-sustaining
 └── Eligible for spinout
 │
 ▼
SPINOUT (optional)
 │
 ├── Independent funding
 └── RTMN retains stake
```
