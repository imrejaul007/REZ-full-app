**Key Finding:** ReZ Merchant already has most features competitors offer. The gaps are in **AI, automation, and specific Indian market needs**.

## CRITICAL GAPS (Must Fix)

### 1. Expiry/Batch Tracking ❌

**Why:** Required for:
- Food safety compliance
- Pharma/cosmetics
- Fresh produce

**Impact:** Cannot serve certain verticals

**Build:** Add expiry date tracking to inventory

---

### 2. Stock Transfer Between Stores ❌

**Why:** Chain stores need to move inventory

**Impact:** Limits multi-location use

**Build:** Transfer request → approval → dispatch → receive

---

### 3. AI Demand Forecasting ❌

**Why:** Competitors have this

**Impact:** Manual ordering, stockouts

**Build:** Connect to rez-ml-models for demand prediction

---

### 4. Customer 360 View ❌

**Why:** Need unified customer profile

**Impact:** Can't personalize properly

**Build:** Aggregate all touchpoints into single view

---

### 5. Tally/Accounting Integration ❌

**Why:** Indian retailers use Tally

**Impact:** Forces manual entry

**Build:** Export to Tally format

---

## HIGH-PRIORITY GAPS

### 6. Social Commerce ❌

**Why:** Instagram, WhatsApp shopping growing

**Impact:** Missing discovery channel

**Build:** Shop from Instagram/WhatsApp links

---

### 7. Multi-language Support ❌

**Why:** India has 20+ languages

**Impact:** Limits adoption

**Build:** i18n for UI, regional language support

---

### 8. Supplier Management ❌

**Why:** NextaBizz needs supplier portal

**Impact:** B2B incomplete

**Build:** Supplier profile, catalog, ordering

---

### 9. GST Auto-filing ❌

**Why:** Manual GSTR-1/GSTR-3B painful

**Impact:** Compliance burden

**Build:** Auto-generate GST returns
