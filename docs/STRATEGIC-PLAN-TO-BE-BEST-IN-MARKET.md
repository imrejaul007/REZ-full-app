## What's Missing: Critical Gaps

### 1. ❌ No Own Delivery Network

**Why it matters:**
- Aggregators (Zomato, Swiggy) take 20-30% commission
- You lose customer relationship
- No real-time control

**What competitors do:**
- DoorDash, UberEats built own fleets
- Toast integrates but doesn't own
- Olo focuses on white-label

**Recommendation:**
```
PHASE 1: White-label delivery partnership
PHASE 2: Build own rider network in key cities
PHASE 3: Micro-fulfillment centers (ghost kitchens)
```

### 2. ❌ Weak Analytics Dashboard

**Why it matters:**
- Operators need real-time visibility
- Current dashboards are basic
- Miss predictive insights

**What you need (based on research):**
| KPI | Real-Time | Predictive | Actionable |
|-----|-----------|------------|------------|
| Sales | ✅ | 🔄 | 🔄 |
| Table Turn Time | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Labor % | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Food Cost % | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Customer LTV | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Churn Risk | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

**Recommendation:**
- Add RevPASH (Revenue per Available Seat Hour)
- Add labor cost tracking
- Add customer lifetime value prediction
- Add churn risk scoring

### 3. ❌ No Financial Services

**Why it matters:**
- Restaurants need working capital
- High churn due to cash flow issues
- Big opportunity for fintech

**What competitors do:**
- Square: Loans, cards, banking
- Toast: Toast Pay, Capital
- Lightspeed: Payments integration

**Recommendation:**
```
ReZ Capital (LENDING)
├── Revenue-based financing
├── Based on POS data
├── No collateral
└── 6-12% interest

ReZ Pay (PAYMENTS)
├── Instant payouts
├── Lower fees than aggregators
├── Unified wallet
└── Business debit card
```

### 4. ❌ Missing Inventory Intelligence

**Why it matters:**
- 4-10% food waste industry average
- Manual tracking is error-prone
- No demand forecasting

**What AI can do:**
- Predict daily demand by item
- Auto-generate purchase orders
- Track waste in real-time
- Suggest menu optimization

**Recommendation:**
```
Smart Inventory (AI-Powered)
├── Demand forecasting
├── Automated reorder triggers
├── Waste tracking
├── Supplier price comparison
└── Menu cost calculator
```

### 5. ❌ No Staff Management

**Why it matters:**
- High turnover in restaurant industry
- Scheduling is manual
- Performance tracking missing

**What's needed:**
- Staff scheduling
- Tip pooling
- Performance analytics
- Payroll integration

### 6. ❌ Weak Customer Data Platform

**Why it matters:**
- Can't personalize without unified data
- Miss cross-restaurant insights
- No 360° customer view

**What you need:**
| Data Source | Current | Needed |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| Orders | ✅ | ✅ |
| Searches | ❌ | ✅ |
| Reviews | ❌ | ✅ |
| Delivery | ❌ | ✅ |
| Loyalty | ✅ | ✅ |
| Social | ❌ | ✅ |
| POS Behavior | ⚠️ Partial | ✅ |
| Feedback | ❌ | ✅ |

### 7. ❌ No Franchise/Hub Model

**Why it matters:**
- Can only grow restaurant by restaurant
- Can't replicate success
- Miss economies of scale

**What competitors do:**
- Lightspeed: Multi-location management
- Toast: Restaurant groups support
- Chipotle: Internal tech for franchises

**Recommendation:**
```
ReZ Franchise Hub
├── Multi-location dashboard
├── Centralized menu management
├── Shared loyalty (cross-brand)
├── Consolidated analytics
├── Centralized procurement (NextaBizz)
└── Franchisee portal
```

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Build Own Delivery Network

**Why:** 25-30% commission to aggregators is unsustainable
**How:** Start with dark kitchens, add delivery partners, scale to own fleet
**Timeline:** Q1-Q4
**Investment:** ₹50-100 Cr over 2 years
**ROI:** Break-even at 10K daily orders

### 2. Launch ReZ Capital

**Why:** Restaurants need working capital, banks don't understand them
**How:** Revenue-based financing using your POS data
**Timeline:** Q2-Q3
**Investment:** ₹20 Cr initial fund
**ROI:** 15-20% yield on loans

### 3. Build Smart Inventory

**Why:** 4-10% food waste = massive savings
**How:** AI demand forecasting + automated ordering
**Timeline:** Q1-Q2
**Investment:** ₹5 Cr
**ROI:** 50% waste reduction = 2-5% margin improvement

### 4. Create Customer Data Platform

**Why:** Personalization requires unified data
**How:** Integrate all touchpoints into single profile
**Timeline:** Q1-Q2
**Investment:** ₹3 Cr
**ROI:** 20% LTV improvement

### 5. Add Financial Services

**Why:** Sticky product, high revenue
**How:** ReZ Pay (payments), ReZ Capital (lending), ReZ Card (debit)
**Timeline:** Q2-Q4
**Investment:** ₹10 Cr
**ROI:** 2% take rate on ₹500 Cr volume = ₹10 Cr ARR
