# SLIDE 16: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Why not build a consumer app like Zomato?

**A:** Apps have 80% churn in 30 days. We don't need an app — **web works on any phone**. This removes the biggest friction point. Our coin system creates the habit, not the app.

### Q: How do you prevent merchant churn?

**A:** **Coins.** When customers earn coins from a merchant, they have an incentive to return. If the merchant leaves ReZ, their customers lose those coins. Merchants can't afford to leave because their customers would follow the coins.

### Q: Why 2% fee instead of free like DotPe?

**A:** Free doesn't create value. Our 2% funds: **24/7 support, AI-powered insights, and customer success**. DotPe's free model means they must monetize elsewhere or through VC subsidies — they don't have a real business.

### Q: What if Zomato or Swiggy copies this?

**A:** They won't. **Aggregators make money on delivery fees**, not loyalty. Adding coin-based loyalty would cannibalize their own commission model. Their incentives are opposite of ours.

### Q: What's your moat?

**A:** **First-mover in coin-based loyalty + focused execution in T2-T3.** But more importantly: data. Once we have 50K merchants and 10M customer scans, we become the de facto SMB intelligence layer.

### Q: Why should we believe in T2-T3?

**A:** **UPI adoption is highest there** (paan shops, vegetable vendors). **Competition is lower** (no Swiggy/Zomato dominance). **Founders have relationships** (500+ SMB network). **Unit economics are better** (lower CAC, higher retention).
