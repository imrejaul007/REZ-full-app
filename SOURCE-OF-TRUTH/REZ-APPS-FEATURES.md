## WHAT IS REZ?

**REZ** is a unified smart savings and growth ecosystem that helps users save money, earn rewards, and discover local businesses through AI-powered intent tracking.

### Core Value Propositions

| For Consumers | For Merchants | For Businesses |
|---------------|---------------|----------------|
| Save money on every purchase | Grow customer base | Intelligent advertising |
| Earn rewards automatically | AI-powered insights | Demand prediction |
| Discover local businesses | Marketing automation | Revenue optimization |
| Student discounts | Inventory management | Real-time analytics |
