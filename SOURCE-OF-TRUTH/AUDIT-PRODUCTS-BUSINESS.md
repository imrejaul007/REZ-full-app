### 3.2 Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning | ReZ Differentiation |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Zomato/Swiggy** | Food delivery, 20-30% commission | No commission, merchant owns data |
| **DotPe** | QR payments only | Coin economy + loyalty + AI |

| **MMT/Goibibo** | Hotel OTAs, 15-25% commission | 10% commission, cross-vertical |
| **Posist/Marg ERP** | Enterprise POS | Affordable, SMB-focused, with ads |
| **Meta/Google Ads** | Digital advertising | Purchase-intent, local, affordable |

### 3.3 Unique Value Proposition

**Core Differentiators:**
1. **Coin Economy Moat** - Network effects increase with every transaction
2. **AI Moat** - 8 agents trained on transaction data
3. **Data Moat** - Transaction history = unmatched consumer insights
4. **T2-T3 Moat** - Focused execution in underserved markets
5. **Flywheel Moat** - Merchants advertise -> Consumers earn -> Merchants win
