## 1. Vision

### AI-First Commerce Platform

REZ transforms commerce through intelligent automation, predictive analytics, and personalized experiences. AI is not a feature -- it is the foundation of every user interaction, business decision, and operational workflow.

**Core Principle:** Every pixel, every API call, every business process should leverage AI to deliver 10x value compared to traditional approaches.

### North Star Vision

> "By 2027, REZ will be the most intelligent commerce platform where AI anticipates user needs before they arise, automates 80% of routine operations, and creates personalized experiences at scale for millions of users."
