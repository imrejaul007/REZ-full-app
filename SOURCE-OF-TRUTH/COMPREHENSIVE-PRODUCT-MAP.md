# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**ReZ** = India AI-powered commerce ecosystem for Metro cities, starting Bangalore.

**RTMN** = **R**e**T**ai**M**oney Net**work** (Holding Company)

**REE** = **R**e**Z **E**conomic Engine = Business Logic Controller

**REE Controls Everything:**
- Tracks ALL transactions
- Earns fees on ALL movements
- Drives engagement
- Powers loyalty/rewards
- Feeds REZ Intelligence
