## ADSQR - What It Really Is

### Definition
**AdsQr** is NOT just tracking. It's a **sampling distribution engine** that:
1. Places QR codes on real-world ads (autos, hoardings, etc.)
2. When scanned → customer gets coins to try products/services FREE

### The Real Flow


### But Different Roles

| Aspect | AdsQr | REZ TRY |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Role** | Distribution channel | Discovery + redemption |
| **QR Codes** | Yes (on ads) | Yes (at merchant) |
| **Coins** | Gives coins | Manages coins |
| **Merchant Discovery** | No | Yes |
| **Trial Booking** | No | Yes |
| **Attribution** | Full tracking | Basic |
