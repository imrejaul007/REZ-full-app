# SALARY SPLIT MODEL

## How Shared Costs Are Divided

### Option 1: Equal Split
| Company | Share | Monthly |
|---------|-------|---------|
| REZ Commerce | 1/6 | ₹X |
| REZ Intelligence | 1/6 | ₹X |
| REZ Media | 1/6 | ₹X |
| StayOwn | 1/6 | ₹X |
| CorpPerks | 1/6 | ₹X |
| RTMN Finance | 1/6 | ₹X |

### Option 2: Revenue-Based Split
| Company | Revenue % | Share |
|---------|---------|-------|
| REZ Commerce | 30% | 30% of shared |
| REZ Intelligence | 15% | 15% of shared |
| REZ Media | 20% | 20% of shared |
| StayOwn | 15% | 15% of shared |
| CorpPerks | 15% | 15% of shared |
| RTMN Finance | 5% | 5% of shared |

### Option 3: Usage-Based Split
| Resource | Split By | Example |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Engineering | Story points | 40/30/20/10 |
| Support | Tickets | Per ticket |
| Infrastructure | API calls | Per call |
| Marketing | Campaigns | Per campaign |
| HR | Headcount | Per hire |
