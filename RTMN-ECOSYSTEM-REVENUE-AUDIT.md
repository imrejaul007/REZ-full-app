```

FLOW:
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Rendez App (user books hotel)
 │

 │    └── Rendez gets 10% = ₹500 commission
 │
 ├──► REZ Intelligence (AI recommendation credited)
 │    └── StayOwn pays 2% = ₹100

 ├──► RTMN Finance (payment processed)

      └── ReZ Economy gets 0.5% = ₹0.25

RENDEZ REVENUE: ₹500 (commission)

STAYOWN PAYS OUT: ₹625 total
```
