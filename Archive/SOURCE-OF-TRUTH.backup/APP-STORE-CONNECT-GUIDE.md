# App Store Connect Submission Guide

## Prerequisites
- Apple Developer Account
- App Store Connect access
- Built iOS app (from EAS)

---

## Step 1: Create App in App Store Connect

### Consumer App (REZ - Shop, Pay, Earn)
1. Go to https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. My Apps → + → New App
3. Fill in:
   - Platforms: iOS
   - Name: REZ - Shop, Pay, Earn
   - Primary Language: English
   - Bundle ID: money.rez.app
   - SKU: REZ-CONSUMER-001

### Merchant App (REZ Merchant)
1. My Apps → + → New App
2. Fill in:
   - Platforms: iOS
   - Name: REZ Merchant
   - Primary Language: English
   - Bundle ID: com.rez.merchant
   - SKU: REZ-MERCHANT-001

---

## Step 2: Fill in App Information

### General Information
- **Category**: Shopping (Primary), Food & Drink (Secondary)
- **Content Rights**: No third-party advertising
- **Age Rating**: 4+ (All Ages)

### App Information
- **Privacy Policy URL**: Required (create privacy policy page)
- **Support URL**: https://rez.money/support
- **Marketing URL**: https://rez.money

### Screenshots Required

#### iPhone 6.5" (1284 x 2778 px)
Upload 5 screenshots showing:
1. Home screen with categories
2. Search results
3. Product detail
4. Cart
5. Order success

#### iPad Pro 12.9" (2048 x 2732 px)
Upload 5 screenshots showing same flows

---

## Step 3: Upload Build

1. In App Store Connect, go to your app
2. Build section → Select a build to add
3. Choose the build uploaded via EAS

---
