## Compliance Notes

### Required Disclosures
- Privacy policy URL (mandatory)
- Age rating explanation available
- In-app purchase info
- Subscription terms if applicable

### Prohibited Content
- No misleading claims ("#1" must be verifiable)
- No comparison to competitors
- No unverified testimonials
- No false urgency ("Only today!")

### Accessibility
- VoiceOver support mentioned
- Dynamic Type compatible
- High contrast mode supported
