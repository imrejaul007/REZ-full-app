## Priority 3: Brand Guidelines

### Logo Usage Rules

#### Primary Logo
- [ ] Full logo (horizontal)
- [ ] Full logo (stacked)
- [ ] Icon only (for app icons, favicons)
- [ ] Clear space: 1x logo height on all sides
- [ ] Minimum size: 32px height (digital), 0.5" (print)

#### Logo Don'ts
- [ ] Do not stretch or distort
- [ ] Do not change colors outside brand palette
- [ ] Do not add effects (shadows, gradients on logo mark)
- [ ] Do not place on busy backgrounds without clear space
- [ ] Do not rotate or flip
- [ ] Do not use unapproved color variants

#### Approved Background Colors for Logo
- White (#FFFFFF)
- Light gray (#F5F5F5)
- Brand primary: [TO BE DEFINED]
- Brand secondary: [TO BE DEFINED]
- Black (#000000) - for reversed/white versions only

6. **Final approval** -> Move to production folder
